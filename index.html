Learn to Play Jolly Roger Blackjack

You were stranded on an island and just heard the pirates are headed for land to steal all the people's gold, silver, and jewels! Luckily, you just found a sailboat! It is your job to make it to land before the pirates do in order to warn the townspeople!!

Quick!! You have to play blackjack against the pirates. If you win a hand, your boat will move forward towards land. If you lose, the pirates will get closer to the treasures!

GOAL: Try to make it to land before the pirates do!
