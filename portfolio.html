HealthTRAX

HealthTRAX is an app that was created to be able to store all of your doctors information in one place. Every doctor seems to have their own website that you need to go to and log-in to be able to see who your doctor is, when you saw them last, and what their contact information is. This is great if you want all of the details of all of your appointments, but sometimes all you need is a simple reminder of when you saw your doctor last, and when you should see them again as well as their simple contact information.

Jolly-Roger-BlackJack

Jolly-Roger-Blackjack is the game of blackjack with a twist. This game has one player versus the computer, and the goal of the game is to get your boat to the land before the pirate gets there in order to warn the townspeople that they are coming. The way to advance your boat is by winning a hand of blackjack.
